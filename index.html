About

Third Pole Group Pvt. Ltd. is a leading media and research company officially registered in Nepal. We are dedicated to advancing Nepal’s media landscape through impactful storytelling, research, and collaboration.

Our team is a blend of journalists, professors, media educators, editors, and entrepreneurs—united by a commitment to quality, innovation, and integrity. We maintain full transparency and regulatory compliance, while driving value for clients, partners, and society.

We are trusted publishers of NewsPolar and enewspolar.com, as well as creators of documentaries, TV and radio programs, and digital campaigns. As leaders in research, training, and cross-cultural collaboration, we are driven by professionalism, transparency, and a vision for a more connected Nepal.
